**Stay Safe Online – Practical Checklist**



**General Safety Practices**

**•** Do not share personal or academic details on unknown websites or forms

* Always keep your passwords strong and unique for different apps
* Never share sensitive information like OTP, passwords, or PIN with anyone
* Be cautious while clicking on links received through messages or emails
* Verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies
* Install apps only from official app stores like Play Store or App Store
* Keep your mobile software and apps updated regularly
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for important activities
* Enable security features like two-factor authentication wherever possible



**UPI \& Financial Safety (Very Important)**

* Remember that entering UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it
* Do not approve unknown payment requests without checking details
* Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources
* Double-check the receiver’s name before confirming any transaction
* Never share OTP or allow screen sharing during payment-related calls
