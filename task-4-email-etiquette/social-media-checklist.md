**Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist**



**Do’s**

* Use strong and secure passwords
* Think before posting anything online
* Respect others’ opinions and privacy
* Share useful and positive content
* Verify information before sharing



**Don’ts**

* Don’t share personal information publicly
* Don’t spread fake news or misinformation
* Don’t post offensive or harmful comments
* Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links
* Don’t waste too much time on social media
